A4 portrait CHAPTER VISUAL SUMMARY page for a Brazilian science textbook. This page goes on the LAST page of the chapter and works as a VISUAL STUDY SUMMARY for 6th-grade students (ages 11-12). It is NOT a flashcard with gimmicky tags — it is an EXPLANATORY SUMMARY where each concept is briefly explained in plain didactic language, the way a clear study sheet does. Friendly didactic infographic style: clean, scannable, modular, color-coded. The student reads it once and understands the chapter; reads it again and remembers it for the test.

PURPOSE AND TONE
- Audience: 11-12 year-old Brazilian student reviewing for a science test.
- Function: explanatory visual summary placed at the end of the chapter.
- Tone: friendly didactic — explains each concept with a short clear sentence. Not editorial-magazine sophistication, not childish cartoon, not gimmicky flashcard. The middle ground: a polished, modern, EXPLANATORY Brazilian school summary sheet.
- Hierarchy must be obvious and the explanations must do the teaching.

- Generous breathing space. Easy hierarchy. Clear modular zones.
