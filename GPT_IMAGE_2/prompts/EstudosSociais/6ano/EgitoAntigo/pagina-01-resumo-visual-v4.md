PREMIUM LAYERING SYSTEM (used throughout)
================================================================
- COLOR-BLOCK ANCHORS: irregular soft-edged painted rectangles (terracota wash, mint wash, sand wash) sitting BEHIND illustrations and groups of bullets — they unify regions without rigid borders.
- BLEEDING IMAGERY: key painterly illustrations EXTEND past their nominal card edges by ~10–20px on one or two sides — the Nile landscape bleeds beyond block 1's frame; the pharaoh bust bleeds beyond block 2's right edge; the scribe extends past block 3.
- ANNOTATION CHIPS on imagery: small white rounded pill-tags (~3px corner radius, 0.5px graphite border) sitting ON TOP of painterly illustrations, with a thin 0.5px dashed graphite line ending in a tiny filled circle on the feature being labeled. Some chips slightly tilted ~2°.
- THIN CURVED CONNECTOR LINES (0.5px graphite, arched, dashed) from outer pictograms toward elements inside illustrations.
- MARKER HIGHLIGHTS (semi-transparent terracota/forest at 30%) painted behind key words in body text — irregular edges like real highlighter pen.
- TINY PICTOGRAMS scattered as marginalia (ankh, scarab, eye of Horus, reed) in painterly line-only.
- SLIGHT ROTATIONS (~2°) on some chips and stamp-style ornaments for dynamic feel.
