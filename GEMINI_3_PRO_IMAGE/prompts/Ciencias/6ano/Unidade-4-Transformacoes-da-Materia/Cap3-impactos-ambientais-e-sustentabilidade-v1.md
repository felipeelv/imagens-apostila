A4 portrait CHAPTER VISUAL SUMMARY page for a Brazilian science textbook. This page is the LAST page of the chapter on "Impactos Ambientais e Sustentabilidade" (environmental impacts and sustainability of synthetic materials), aimed at 6th-grade students (ages 11-12) reviewing for a science test. Friendly didactic infographic style — clean, scannable, modular, color-coded — but with subtle layout variations (slight card stagger, organic leaf accents, redesigned key-idea badge) that bring more visual life to the page without sacrificing clarity.

- Tone: friendly didactic, with a slightly more organic/dynamic layout than the previous chapters. Each concept is briefly explained.
