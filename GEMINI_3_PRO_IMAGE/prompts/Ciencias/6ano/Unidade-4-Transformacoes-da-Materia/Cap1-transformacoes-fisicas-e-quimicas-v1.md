# Pedagogical Science Poster — Brazilian Textbook (6th grade)
# Subject: Ciências · 6º ano EF · Unidade 4 · Capítulo 1
# Topic: Transformações Físicas e Químicas

Create an A4 vertical pedagogical summary poster for a Brazilian elementary school science textbook, summarizing the chapter "Transformações Físicas e Químicas". The poster is a one-page visual summary that helps an 11-12 year-old student remember the core concepts of the chapter at a glance. Editorial science illustration aesthetic, clean and modern, inspired by National Geographic / Scientific American educational posters, but tailored for younger students.

## CANVAS AND BACKGROUND

- A4 portrait, aspect ratio 3:4.
- Background: PURE WHITE (#FFFFFF). FLAT WHITE. No paper texture, no vintage grain, no cream tone, no vignette, no gradient. The white must be true RGB white so the page prints cleanly on A4.
- Generous breathing space between modules. Comfortable margins.

## TYPOGRAPHY

- Main title "TRANSFORMAÇÕES" — condensed slab-serif, distressed/textured uppercase, very dark charcoal #2D2D2D, gigantic (occupies full width of header zone). Subtle ink-press texture only on this title (no grunge anywhere else).
- Subtitle "Físicas e Químicas" — slab-serif regular weight, sentence case, terracotta #C25B3F, smaller than title.
- Pergunta-problema — serif italic, deep teal #1F4D5A, large size, two lines maximum.
- Section headers (TRANSFORMAÇÃO FÍSICA / TRANSFORMAÇÃO QUÍMICA / AS 5 EVIDÊNCIAS / ESSE FOI O "CARA" / PARA NÃO ESQUECER) — sans-serif Black uppercase, white text on terracotta #C25B3F brushstroke banner.
- Body text — sans-serif regular (Roboto / Inter), dark charcoal #2D2D2D.
- Cientista name — serif bold uppercase.
- Versículo — serif italic, deep teal #1F4D5A, smaller.
- NEVER use Comic Sans, NEVER handwriting/script, NEVER 3D extruded effects.

## COLOR PALETTE (clean lab/science feel — replaces savanna)

- Background: pure white #FFFFFF
- Primary accent: terracotta burnt orange #C25B3F (brushstroke banners, accent details, callout dots)
- Secondary: deep teal / petrol blue #1F4D5A (icons, scientist box, contrast typography)
- Tertiary: mustard gold #D4A017 (highlight markers, evidence badges accent ring, versículo vertical bar)
- Neutral gray: #E8E8E8 (very subtle dividers only)
- Text: dark charcoal #2D2D2D (NEVER pure black)

## LAYOUT (top to bottom — 6 zones)

### ZONE 1 — TOP HEADER STRIP (~12% of height)
- Left two-thirds: distressed slab-serif title "TRANSFORMAÇÕES" gigantic, charcoal. Below it, smaller "Físicas e Químicas" in terracotta.
- Right one-third: small black rounded chip with white sans-serif uppercase text: "CIÊNCIAS · 6º ANO · UNIDADE 4 · CAP 1"

### ZONE 2 — CENTRAL HERO (~28% of height)
- Photorealistic editorial illustration of TWO eggs side by side on a clean white surface, NO plate, NO table, just resting on white:
  - LEFT EGG: a raw egg cracked open on a small white surface, yolk and clear white visible, glossy and translucent — represents reversibility (state changed but composition same).
  - RIGHT EGG: a peeled boiled egg, sliced in half showing solid white and firm yellow yolk — represents irreversible chemical transformation (new substance formed).
- BETWEEN the two eggs (occupying the upper-center space), the pergunta-problema in large serif italic deep teal, two lines:
  "Se um ovo pudesse voltar a ser cru, o que isso diria sobre a natureza do cozimento?"
- Thin terracotta callout line from each egg pointing to a small label box (no border, just text):
  - LEFT label: "ESTADO REVERSÍVEL" (uppercase bold) + below in regular: "formato pode mudar, composição não"
  - RIGHT label: "NOVA SUBSTÂNCIA" (uppercase bold) + below in regular: "não volta atrás"

### ZONE 3 — TWO CONTRASTING COLUMNS (~22% of height)
Two columns of equal width, side by side, with a thin vertical gray divider between them.

LEFT COLUMN — header "TRANSFORMAÇÃO FÍSICA" in white on terracotta brushstroke banner:
- Definition (sans-serif body, charcoal): "Mudança que NÃO forma nova substância. A composição permanece."
- Three small monoline icon illustrations in deep teal (#1F4D5A), arranged horizontally with a short label below each (sans-serif uppercase small):
  - Icon: ice cube melting with water drop → "GELO DERRETE"
  - Icon: scissors cutting paper → "PAPEL CORTADO"
  - Icon: spoon stirring sugar in glass → "AÇÚCAR DISSOLVE"

RIGHT COLUMN — header "TRANSFORMAÇÃO QUÍMICA" in white on terracotta brushstroke banner:
- Definition (sans-serif body, charcoal): "Mudança que FORMA nova substância. A composição muda."
- Three small monoline icon illustrations in deep teal, arranged horizontally with label below:
  - Icon: egg in pan with heat lines → "OVO COZINHA"
  - Icon: paper with flame → "PAPEL QUEIMA"
  - Icon: rusty nail with rust flakes → "PREGO ENFERRUJA"

### ZONE 4 — EVIDENCES STRIP (~14% of height)
- Full-width terracotta brushstroke banner with white text uppercase: "AS 5 EVIDÊNCIAS DE TRANSFORMAÇÃO QUÍMICA"
- Below the banner, a SINGLE ROW of FIVE evenly-spaced circular badges. Each badge:
  - Outer ring: mustard gold #D4A017 thin (~3px)
  - Inner circle: deep teal #1F4D5A solid background
  - White minimal monoline icon centered inside (specific per badge)
  - Below the badge, sans-serif Black uppercase small label in charcoal:
    - Badge 1 — palette/swatch icon → "MUDANÇA DE COR"
    - Badge 2 — bubbles rising icon → "LIBERAÇÃO DE GÁS"
    - Badge 3 — thermometer icon → "MUDANÇA DE TEMPERATURA"
    - Badge 4 — nose with wave lines icon → "MUDANÇA DE CHEIRO"
    - Badge 5 — beaker with sediment at bottom → "FORMAÇÃO DE PRECIPITADO"
- Each badge appears EXACTLY ONCE. Render only once. Count: 5 badges total.

### ZONE 5 — BOTTOM ROW (3 columns) (~20% of height)

LEFT COLUMN (~30% of width) — "ESSE FOI O 'CARA'":
- Small terracotta brushstroke header: ESSE FOI O "CARA"
- Vintage woodcut / engraving style portrait of Jöns Jacob Berzelius (Swedish chemist, early 19th century), monotone deep teal #1F4D5A on white, head-and-shoulders, dignified. Style reference: 1800s scientific journal engraving with cross-hatching.
- Below the portrait, sans-serif:
  - "JÖNS JACOB BERZELIUS" (bold uppercase)
  - "1779–1848 · QUÍMICO SUECO" (regular, small caps)
  - One body line: "Ajudou a criar a linguagem precisa da Química moderna."

CENTER COLUMN (~40% of width) — "PARA NÃO ESQUECER":
- Terracotta brushstroke header: "PARA NÃO ESQUECER"
- Five short bullet lines, sans-serif, charcoal, with mustard gold square bullet markers:
  - Física não muda a composição
  - Química forma nova substância
  - 5 evidências ajudam a decidir
  - Justificar > chutar
  - Observar antes de concluir

RIGHT COLUMN (~30% of width) — Versículo:
- Vertical mustard gold #D4A017 bar (~5px wide) on the left edge of the box
- Inside the box, sans-serif Black uppercase: "DANIEL 6:4"
- Below it, serif italic deep teal in two lines:
  "Integridade científica é descrever o que se observa, não o que conviria concluir."

### ZONE 6 — FOOTER STRIP (~4% of height)
- A thin terracotta horizontal rule across the page
- Centered tiny sans-serif uppercase charcoal: "CIÊNCIAS · 6º ANO · UNIDADE 4 · CAPÍTULO 1 · TRANSFORMAÇÕES FÍSICAS E QUÍMICAS"

## SUBTLE BACKGROUND DOODLES (very faint, decorative only)
Tiny gray (#E8E8E8) line-art doodles scattered very sparsely in empty corners of the page — never overlapping content, never competing visually:
- A small test tube outline
- A small magnifying glass outline
- A few tiny dots
Only 4-5 doodles total in the entire page. They should be barely noticeable. Background must read as predominantly white space.

## NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS — DO NOT INCLUDE

- NO cream, beige, ivory or yellow background. Background is pure white only.
- NO paper texture, no parchment, no vintage grain, no aged effect over the whole page.
- NO chemical equations, NO molecular structures, NO formulas.
- NO mascot, NO cartoon character, NO student illustration.
- NO drop shadows on cards. Flat clean design only.
- NO duplicate badges. Render each of the 5 evidence badges exactly ONCE.
- NO ornamental corners, NO frames around the whole poster.
- NO QR code, NO logo, NO publisher mark.
- NO chemistry lab photos, NO microscope imagery, NO molecule renders.
- NO 3D effects, NO embossed text, NO neon, NO glow.
- NO grunge or distressed effect anywhere except the main title "TRANSFORMAÇÕES".
- NO Comic Sans, NO handwritten script fonts.
- NO clipart-style egg cartoons. The eggs must look photorealistic and editorial.
- NO yellow or savanna palette. The earlier reference image (cheetah poster) had warm cream background — this poster must NOT.
