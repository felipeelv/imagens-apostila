# Pedagogical Science Infographic — Brazilian Textbook
# Topic: Transformações Físicas e Químicas (chapter conceptual summary)

Create an A4 portrait pedagogical INFOGRAPHIC that visually summarizes the science chapter "Transformações Físicas e Químicas" for a Brazilian student aged 11-12. Editorial science infographic style — like a clean modern science magazine spread or a museum interpretive panel. The poster focuses PURELY on the conceptual content. NO grade/year/unit labels. NO scientist biographies. NO bullet-point summary boxes. The student should be able to look at this poster and "get" the chapter at a glance.

## CANVAS AND BACKGROUND

- Aspect ratio 3:4 (A4 portrait)
- Background: PURE WHITE #FFFFFF, completely flat, NO texture, NO grain, NO cream, NO yellow tint, NO vignette
- Generous margins and breathing space

## TYPOGRAPHY

- Main title: ONE WORD "TRANSFORMAÇÕES" rendered as a single unbroken word (NEVER "TRANS FORMAÇÕES" with a space). Condensed slab-serif distressed uppercase, charcoal #2D2D2D. If it doesn't fit, scale DOWN — never break the word.
- Subtitle "Físicas e Químicas": serif italic, terracotta #C25B3F
- Pergunta-problema: serif italic, deep teal #1F4D5A, large, integrated near the eggs
- "FÍSICA" / "QUÍMICA" big labels: sans-serif Black uppercase
- Body text and definitions: sans-serif regular (Roboto/Inter), charcoal
- All Portuguese accents must render exactly (õ, ç, á, é, ã, ó, ú, í, ê)

## COLOR PALETTE — encoded contrast (cool vs warm)

Color encodes the conceptual contrast: cool/calm = same matter (física); warm/active = new substance (química).

- Background: pure white #FFFFFF
- FÍSICA side: cool deep teal / petrol #1F4D5A
- QUÍMICA side: warm terracotta #C25B3F
- Evidence badges accent: mustard gold #D4A017 (rings, highlights)
- Text: charcoal #2D2D2D (never pure black)
- Subtle dividers: light gray #E8E8E8

## LAYOUT (top to bottom)

### ZONE 1 — TITLE (top, ~8% of page)

- "TRANSFORMAÇÕES" as a single solid word, distressed slab-serif uppercase, charcoal, centered
- Below: "Físicas e Químicas" in serif italic, terracotta, smaller

NO chip, NO subtitle banner, NO grade/year metadata anywhere on the page.

### ZONE 2 — HERO IMAGE WITH PERGUNTA-PROBLEMA (~38% of page) — DOMINANT VISUAL

This zone is the visual heart of the page. The eggs are LARGE, photorealistic, beautifully lit on pure white surface.

- Centered composition: TWO eggs side by side with comfortable space between them.
  - LEFT egg: photorealistic raw egg, shell cracked open into two clean halves, golden yolk and translucent clear white visible, ivory shell. NOT fried, NOT splashed — a clean fresh cracked egg.
  - RIGHT egg: photorealistic boiled egg, peeled, sliced cleanly in half showing solid opaque white and firm yellow yolk (cross-section like a deviled egg). Clean and editorial.
- BETWEEN and slightly above the eggs (in the upper hero zone), the pergunta-problema floats in serif italic deep teal, two lines, large:
  "Se um ovo pudesse voltar a ser cru, o que isso diria sobre a natureza do cozimento?"
- BELOW EACH EGG, a single small label (NO duplications):
  - Below LEFT egg, in cool teal sans-serif Black: "VOLTA A SER CRU?" then in regular charcoal: "Sim. Só mudou de estado."
  - Below RIGHT egg, in warm terracotta sans-serif Black: "VOLTA A SER CRU?" then in regular charcoal: "Não. Virou outra substância."

### ZONE 3 — DECISION SPLIT (~28% of page) — REPLACES THE TWO STATIC SUMMARY COLUMNS

This zone is structured as a VISUAL DECISION, not as a side-by-side definition table.

- TOP CENTER of the zone, a horizontal navigator question in sans-serif charcoal Black uppercase: "FORMOU UMA NOVA SUBSTÂNCIA?"
- Just below, a horizontal divider with TWO arrows splitting outward:
  - LEFT arrow points to "NÃO" — leads to the FÍSICA block on the left
  - RIGHT arrow points to "SIM" — leads to the QUÍMICA block on the right

LEFT BLOCK (under the "NÃO" arrow), color tinted very pale teal:
- Large diagonal label: "TRANSFORMAÇÃO FÍSICA" in deep teal, sans-serif Black uppercase
- One short line below: "A matéria muda de forma, estado ou aparência. A composição continua a mesma."
- Three monoline TEAL icons arranged horizontally with a small caption below each:
  - ice cube melting → "GELO DERRETE"
  - scissors and paper → "PAPEL CORTADO"
  - spoon stirring sugar in a glass → "AÇÚCAR DISSOLVE"

RIGHT BLOCK (under the "SIM" arrow), color tinted very pale terracotta:
- Large diagonal label: "TRANSFORMAÇÃO QUÍMICA" in terracotta, sans-serif Black uppercase
- One short line below: "A matéria vira uma substância NOVA. A composição muda."
- Three monoline TERRACOTTA icons arranged horizontally with a small caption below each:
  - egg in pan with heat lines → "OVO COZINHA"
  - paper with small flame → "PAPEL QUEIMA"
  - rusty nail → "PREGO ENFERRUJA"

### ZONE 4 — DETECTIVE TOOLKIT: 5 EVIDENCES (~22% of page)

- Centered headline in charcoal, sans-serif Black uppercase, large: "COMO PERCEBER UMA TRANSFORMAÇÃO QUÍMICA?"
- Below it, in serif italic charcoal, smaller, centered: "5 pistas que indicam que surgiu uma substância nova"
- Below the headline, ONE single horizontal row of FIVE evenly-spaced cards.

Each card is composed of:
1. Top: a circular badge — outer thin ring mustard gold #D4A017 (~3px), inner solid deep teal #1F4D5A, centered minimal white monoline icon
2. Middle: bold uppercase label in charcoal sans-serif Black
3. Bottom: a tiny example phrase in serif italic in muted gray (#666)

The five cards (each rendered EXACTLY ONCE — count: 5 total):
- Card 1 — icon: paint palette / color swatches → "MUDANÇA DE COR" → "prego ganha cor laranja"
- Card 2 — icon: rising bubbles → "LIBERAÇÃO DE GÁS" → "vinagre e bicarbonato borbulham"
- Card 3 — icon: thermometer → "MUDANÇA DE TEMPERATURA" → "queimar esquenta"
- Card 4 — icon: nose with three small wave lines → "MUDANÇA DE CHEIRO" → "leite azeda"
- Card 5 — icon: beaker with sediment at the bottom → "FORMAÇÃO DE PRECIPITADO" → "líquido vira sólido"

Below the cards, centered, in serif italic small charcoal: "Uma pista sozinha pode enganar — observe o conjunto."

### ZONE 5 — FOOTER VERSE (~4% of page)

- A thin terracotta horizontal rule across the page
- Below it, two short centered lines:
  - "DANIEL 6:4" in sans-serif Black uppercase small, charcoal
  - "Integridade científica é descrever o que se observa, não o que conviria concluir." in serif italic small, deep teal

## NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS — STRICT

- NO grade/year/unit chip or label anywhere ("CIÊNCIAS", "6º ANO", "UNIDADE 4", "CAPÍTULO 1" — none of these strings appear).
- NO scientist portrait, NO scientist biography, NO "Esse foi o cara" section.
- NO "Para não esquecer" bullet summary box.
- NO yellow, cream, ivory or beige background — pure white only.
- NO paper texture, vintage grain, parchment effect, sepia tone.
- NO chemical equations, NO molecular formulas, NO atomic diagrams.
- NO mascot, NO student character, NO cartoon figures.
- NO duplicate cards/badges. Each evidence card appears exactly ONCE. Total: 5 cards.
- NO duplicate egg labels (do NOT repeat "VOLTA A SER CRU?" three times under the same egg).
- NO drop shadows on the cards. Flat clean design.
- NO ornamental corners, NO decorative frames around the page.
- NO QR codes, NO logos, NO publisher marks.
- NO 3D, NO embossed text, NO neon, NO glow.
- NO grunge effect anywhere except the main title.
- NO clipart-style egg — must look photographic and editorial.
- NEVER split "TRANSFORMAÇÕES" into "TRANS FORMAÇÕES". Keep it as ONE unbroken word, single horizontal baseline.
- NO Comic Sans, NO handwriting fonts, NO script.
