**INSTRUÇÃO DE RACIOCÍNIO INICIAL**

Before generating the image, think step by step about the spatial layout. The composition is a vertical 3:4 educational visual summary for a Brazilian student in 4º ano EF (a 9-10 year old child). The page must feel energetic, modern, illustrated and engaging — like a Khan Academy or Duolingo illustration, NOT like a textbook page or a calligraphy poster. Plan placement, color distribution, typographic hierarchy and reading flow first; only then render the final image. Text rendering must be precise: every Portuguese word must include correct diacritics. Verify diacritics on words such as "concordância", "está", "é", "ação", "não", "número", "pessoa", "pronome", "também", "você", "Lucas".

The page must be readable in 2 to 3 minutes. Use Roboto for ALL text.

**ESTILO VISUAL GERAL**

Modern editorial illustration in the spirit of Khan Academy / Duolingo / Brainscape — vibrant, friendly, slightly playful but not childish. Hand-drawn outlines combined with saturated color fills that have very subtle painterly texture (gouache-like, gentle brush variation, NOT perfectly flat vector fill). Visible paper texture on a cream background with a gentle warm vignette (slightly brighter at the center, slightly cooler at the corners). Soft drop shadows under cards and arrows. Outlines in dark navy ink with slight weight variation, never pure black, never perfectly uniform.

Saturated palette (NOT pastel, NOT neon): electric blue, emerald green, golden yellow, grape purple, tangerine orange, dark red. Each color anchors one branch (one arrow + its destination card). No color dominates more than 25% of the page.

Illustrations whenever possible show MINI SCENES from a 9-10 year old Brazilian student's daily life: backpack, classroom, friends, books, notebook, park, family — never abstract icons floating alone. Show the concept through the scene, not just label it.

Style restriction: characters must look like real-ish stylized 9-10 year old students with moderate proportions and friendly faces — no big-headed mascots, no exaggerated bouncy proportions, no Cartoon Network look.

- Has a thick body in saturated color (one color per arrow), with subtle hand-painted texture.
- Has a clearly defined arrowhead pointing AWAY from the central circle.
- Has a gently curved or slightly tapered body — never a straight rigid rectangle. Width varies organically like a brush stroke.
- Has a soft hand-drawn dark navy outline with slight weight variation, plus a soft drop shadow.
- Carries a short uppercase label written ON the arrow itself in white Roboto Bold, with a very subtle slight tilt to feel less mechanical.
- Connects the central circle to one of six illustrated cards positioned around the page in six distinct directions: upper-left, upper-right, right, lower-right, lower-left, left.

- Qualquer fonte serifada, manuscrita, caligráfica ou de fantasia. Apenas Roboto.

- Mais de seis cards principais.
- Marcadores de posição "11h", "1h", "3h" ou qualquer indicação literal de horas de relógio na imagem.

- Texto em idioma diferente de português brasileiro.
- Saturação visual: a página deve respirar.
