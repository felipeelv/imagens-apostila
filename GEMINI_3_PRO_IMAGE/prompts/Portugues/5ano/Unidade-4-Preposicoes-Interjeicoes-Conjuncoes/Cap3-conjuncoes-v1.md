**INSTRUÇÃO DE RACIOCÍNIO INICIAL**

Before generating the image, think step by step about the spatial layout. The composition is a vertical 3:4 educational visual summary for a Brazilian student in 5º ano EF (a 10-11 year old child). The page must feel energetic, modern, illustrated and engaging — like a Khan Academy or Duolingo illustration, NOT like a textbook page or a calligraphy poster. Plan placement, color distribution, typographic hierarchy and reading flow first; only then render the final image. Text rendering must be precise: every Portuguese word must include correct diacritics. Verify diacritics on words such as "conjunção", "ligação", "está", "é", "não", "também", "porém", "porque", "condição", "finalidade", "Provérbios".

The page must be readable in 2 to 3 minutes. Use Roboto for ALL text.

---

**TIPOGRAFIA**

Use the **Roboto** font family for ALL text. No serif, no script, no calligraphy.
- Roboto Black for the central concept word and uppercase station titles.
- Roboto Bold for arrow labels (white on the arrow body).
- Roboto Medium for example phrases and short explanations.
- Roboto Regular Italic for marginal notes.
- NO asterisks, hashtags or markdown markers rendered literally.

---

**ESTILO VISUAL GERAL**

Modern editorial illustration in the spirit of Khan Academy / Duolingo / Brainscape — vibrant, friendly, slightly playful but not childish. Hand-drawn outlines combined with saturated color fills with subtle painterly texture. **Pure white background — NO cream tint, NO vignette, NO paper texture, NO background color fill.** A4 print-ready. Cards remain distinct on white via colored accent stripe + soft drop shadow + dark-navy outline. Outlines in dark navy ink with slight weight variation, never pure black.

Saturated palette (NOT pastel, NOT neon): emerald green, coral red, electric blue, golden yellow, grape purple, dark red. One color per branch. No color over 25% of the page.

Mini-scenes show 10-11 year old Brazilian students in everyday situations: studying, talking with friends, sports, classroom, family. Connectives live in dialogue and reasoning — show students reasoning together.

Style restriction: characters must look like real-ish stylized 10-11 year old students with moderate proportions and friendly faces — no big-headed mascots, no Cartoon Network look.

- Thick body in saturated color, with subtle hand-painted texture.
- Clearly defined arrowhead pointing AWAY from the central circle.
- Gently curved or tapered body.
- Soft hand-drawn dark navy outline + soft drop shadow.
- Short uppercase label in white Roboto Bold ON the arrow.
- Connects the central circle to one of six illustrated cards: upper-left, upper-right, right, lower-right, lower-left, left.

- Setas vetoriais chapadas, geométricas e duras, sem textura.
- Selos históricos, carimbos, retratos antigos. NÃO incluir Othon Moacyr Garcia nem qualquer figura histórica.

- Cards repetidos ou duplicados — cada card deve aparecer uma única vez.
- Mais de seis cards principais.

- Texturas de papel, vinhetas, gradientes.
- Texto em idioma diferente de português brasileiro.
