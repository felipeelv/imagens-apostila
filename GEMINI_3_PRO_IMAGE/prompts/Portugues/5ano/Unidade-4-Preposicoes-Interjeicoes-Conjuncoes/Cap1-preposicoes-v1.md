**INSTRUÇÃO DE RACIOCÍNIO INICIAL**

Before generating the image, think step by step about the spatial layout. The composition is a vertical 3:4 educational visual summary for a Brazilian student in 5º ano EF (a 10-11 year old child). The page must feel energetic, modern, illustrated and engaging — like a Khan Academy or Duolingo illustration, NOT like a textbook page or a calligraphy poster. Plan placement, color distribution, typographic hierarchy and reading flow first; only then render the final image. Text rendering must be precise: every Portuguese word must include correct diacritics. Verify diacritics on words such as "preposição", "ligação", "está", "é", "não", "relação", "direção", "finalidade", "companhia", "instrumento", "Provérbios".

The page must be readable in 2 to 3 minutes. Use Roboto for ALL text.

**TIPOGRAFIA — IMPORTANTE**

Use the **Roboto** font family for ALL text in the image. No serif, no script, no calligraphy.
- Roboto Black for the central concept word and uppercase station titles.

**ESTILO VISUAL GERAL**

Modern editorial illustration in the spirit of Khan Academy / Duolingo / Brainscape — vibrant, friendly, slightly playful but not childish. Hand-drawn outlines combined with saturated color fills that have very subtle painterly texture (gouache-like, gentle brush variation, NOT perfectly flat vector fill). **Pure white background — NO cream tint, NO vignette, NO paper texture, NO background color fill of any kind.** The page is designed for A4 print and must keep the background as solid white so it does not consume printer ink. Cards must remain clearly distinct on the white via their colored accent stripe, soft drop shadow and dark-navy outline. Outlines in dark navy ink with slight weight variation, never pure black.

Saturated palette (NOT pastel, NOT neon): electric blue, emerald green, golden yellow, coral red, grape purple, dark red. Each color anchors one branch (one arrow + its destination card). No color dominates more than 25% of the page.

Illustrations whenever possible show MINI SCENES from a 10-11 year old Brazilian student's daily life: backpack, classroom, friend, school, park, family, books — never abstract icons floating alone.

Style restriction: characters must look like real-ish stylized 10-11 year old students with moderate proportions and friendly faces — no big-headed mascots, no exaggerated bouncy proportions, no Cartoon Network look.
