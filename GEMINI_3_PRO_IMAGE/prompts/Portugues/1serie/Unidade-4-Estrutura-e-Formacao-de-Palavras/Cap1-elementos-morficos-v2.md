**INSTRUÇÃO DE RACIOCÍNIO INICIAL**

Before generating the image, think step by step about the spatial layout. The composition is a vertical 3:4 educational study poster for a Brazilian student in 1ª série EM (a 15-16 year old learner approaching ENEM/vestibular). The visual reference is **modern study aesthetic** — like a premium Notion template, a Descomplica/Hexag online course card, a TED-Ed slide, or a vibrant "study with me" Pinterest poster. Mature but **alive, energetic, vibrant** — something a teen would want on their wall. NOT corporate handout. NOT formal academic manual. NOT marker sketchnote (that's EF2). NOT Khan/Duolingo with mascot (that's EF1). Plan placement, color distribution, typographic hierarchy and reading flow first; only then render the final image. Text rendering must be precise: every Portuguese word must include correct diacritics. Verify diacritics on words such as "mórfico", "morfema", "fonema", "está", "é", "não", "número", "radical", "temática", "desinência", "afixo", "Provérbios", "infelizmente", "cantávamos".

The page is a study poster — readable in 3 to 5 minutes for revision.

**TIPOGRAFIA**

Use a confident **modern editorial sans-serif with personality** (Plus Jakarta Sans, Manrope, Space Grotesk, or Roboto in heavy weights). NO formal serif. NO marker handwritten. NO traditional cursive ANYWHERE.

- **Central title**: bold sans-serif **BLACK UPPERCASE**, very large, strong visual presence. Display weight, tight letter spacing.
- **Conceptual subtitle**: sans-serif Medium italic OK, slightly smaller.
- **Card numerals (I, II, III...)**: oversized **bold sans-serif Roman numerals** placed inside circular badges with the card's accent color, with a soft drop shadow for depth.
- **Card titles**: bold sans-serif **UPPERCASE in white**, on top of a colored solid header strip (color block).
- **Body text**: clean sans-serif Regular/Medium in dark navy/near-black. Bold key terms in the card's accent color. Selected key concepts wrapped in **soft marker-highlight backgrounds** (digital highlighter style — translucent pastel rectangle behind the word, in the card's accent tint).
- **Morphological notation** (`cant- + -a- + -va- + -mos`): monospace font, slightly larger for emphasis, in the card's accent color.

NO cursive ANYWHERE.

**ESTILO VISUAL GERAL**

Modern study-poster aesthetic — **energetic, vibrant, alive**. Premium visual quality with personality. Dark navy outlines with consistent weight. Card shapes are **rectangles with generous rounded corners (~14 px radius)**, like modern app cards. Each card has:
- A **solid colored header strip** at the top (full color block, NOT just a thin accent line) with the card title in bold WHITE text.
- A **circular badge with the Roman numeral** floating slightly off the upper-left corner of the header (white circle with colored border, soft drop shadow, large bold numeral inside in card's accent color).
- White interior body.
- Subtle drop shadow under the entire card (gentle, modern).

**Pure white background — NO cream tint, NO grid pattern, NO paper texture, NO vignette.** A4 print-ready.

**Decorative geometric accents** sprinkled subtly around and between cards — these are CRITICAL for the energetic feel:
- 4-6 small filled colored circles (random palette colors, ~10 px diameter, soft drop shadow)
- 2-3 thin diagonal lines in pencil-gray or in pastel colors
- 1-2 small "+" marks in lime or violet
- 1 dotted cluster in magenta
- 1 small outlined hollow square in cobalt
- 1-2 tiny colored stars

These add MOVEMENT and ENERGY without competing with cards. They are the page's "personality sparks." Never overlap text or arrows.

**Vibrant modern palette** (saturated but harmonized — NOT neon, NOT pastel chapado, NOT corporate sober):
- **electric cobalt blue** (vivid, confident)
- **vivid magenta** (energetic pink-red)
- **lime green** (fresh, modern)
- **mango orange** (warm, vibrant)
- **deep violet** (sophisticated)
- **golden yellow** (bright, friendly)

Each color anchors one card. The palette feels like a 2024-2025 design system — Notion, Figma, Spotify Wrapped, modern study apps.

NO mascot characters. NO illustrated mini-scenes. NO drawings of children or daily-life vignettes. Cards contain text + structural diagrams + the small decorative accents.

**RESTRIÇÕES NEGATIVAS**

Não incluir:
- Mascote-personagem ou ilustração de estudante.
- Mini-cenas ilustradas dentro dos cards.
- Cards sem header colorido sólido. CADA card tem header em color block (full block, not thin line) + numeral em badge circular flutuante.
- Cursiva, manuscrito, marker-handwritten ou caligráfica em qualquer texto. APENAS sans-serif moderno.
- Fonte serifada formal.
- Layout em mapa-mental radial com setas saindo de um centro.
- Setas pintadas espessas tipo pincelada (estilo EF1).
- Setas finas marker em ângulos orgânicos (estilo EF2).
- Estética "documento corporativo" / "manual escolar antigo" / "handout sóbrio monocromático" — a página deve ser MODERNA, ENERGÉTICA, VIVA, com cara de design de 2024-2025, algo que um adolescente colaria na parede.
- Selos históricos. NÃO incluir Antenor Nascentes nem qualquer figura histórica.
- Marcas comerciais (Anglo, Bernoulli, Poliedro, Etapa, Descomplica, Hexag, Notion, Figma, etc.), logotipos, propagandas, marca d'água.
- Asteriscos, hashtags, colchetes ou marcadores markdown literais aparecendo no texto.
- Marcadores de posição "11h", "1h", etc.
- Fundo de cor (creme, bege). Branco puro.
- Texturas de papel, vinhetas, gradientes pesados no fundo.
- Cards repetidos. EXATAMENTE 6 cards.
- Paleta sóbria/corporativa (navy puro, burgundy escuro, slate gray morto). USE a paleta vibrante moderna: cobalt blue, magenta, lime green, mango orange, deep violet, golden yellow.
- Ausência de elementos decorativos geométricos (pontos coloridos, traços, "+"). A página DEVE ter "personality sparks" espalhados.
- Conteúdo proibido pelo blueprint: processos de formação de palavras (derivação/composição) como conteúdo central, conjugação verbal completa, sistema pronominal, lista extensa de radicais decorativa, etimologia histórica com mudança fonética.
- Texto em idioma diferente de português brasileiro.

A página deve ser claramente identificável como material vibrante e moderno de Língua Portuguesa de 1ª série EM brasileiro — algo que um adolescente de 15-16 anos veria como bonito o suficiente para colar na parede do quarto.
